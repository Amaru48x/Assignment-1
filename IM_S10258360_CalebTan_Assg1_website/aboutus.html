    I'm Lovin' It - Our Singapore Story

    Our Golden Arches first set foot in Singapore more than 40 years ago; in 1979. Today, more than 135 McDonald's restaurants island-wide serve 6.5 million customers every month.


    Our Values in Action

    From the start, we've been committed to doing the right thing. In accordance with our McDonald's values in Singapore and around the globe, we're here to make a difference.

    Our Commitment to Quality

    At McDonald's we take great care to ensure that what we serve every day is safe, of great quality and produced in a responsible way. That means we use 100% real beef patties seasoned with just a pinch of salt and pepper. It means our Egg McMuffin® is made with a freshly cracked egg. And, our Filet-O-Fish® is made with 100% Pollock fish sourced from sustainably certified fisheries. It means we use whole chicken thigh in our McSpicy® and our chicken.
